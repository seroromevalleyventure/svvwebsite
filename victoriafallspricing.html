Disclaimer: For US citizens visiting Zimbabwe, a visa is required, costing around $30 (single entry), $45 (double entry), and $55 (multiple entry), payable in cash (USD) on arrival or via the eVisa system. KAZA Univisa ($50) is also accepted.

US dollars are widely accepted. Please carry small, clean bills.
