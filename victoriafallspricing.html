Disclaimer: For US citizens visiting Zimbabwe, a visa is required, costing around $30 for single entry, $45 for double entry, and $55 for multiple entry, payable in cash (USD) on arrival at ports or online via the eVisa system, though processing/service fees add to the total cost for eVisa applications. The KAZA Univisa ($50) is also an option for Zambia/Zimbabwe travel. US dollars are widely accepted for tourism expenses; bring small, crisp bills ($1, $5, $10, $20) as change for larger denominations can be difficult.
