Privacy Policy

Your trust and privacy are our priority. This policy explains how Serorome Valley Ventures collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect your name, contact details, travel preferences, and payment information when you make a booking or inquiry.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to arrange travel services, process payments, and communicate relevant updates regarding your booking.

3. Data Security

We use secure digital systems and encrypted communication to safeguard your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We only share necessary travel details with trusted partners such as airlines, lodges, and tour operators — all bound by confidentiality agreements.

5. POPI Compliance

Serorome Valley Ventures complies fully with South Africa’s Protection of Personal Information (POPI) Act. You may request access to or deletion of your data at any time.

6. Contact Us

If you have privacy-related concerns, please email our Data Officer at info@seroromevalleyventures.com.

By using our website and services, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
