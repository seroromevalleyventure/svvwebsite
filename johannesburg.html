Johannesburg Experience

Johannesburg, known as the City of Gold, is South Africa’s largest city and the economic powerhouse of the continent—home to Africa’s biggest financial district, Sandton. Located in the heart of Gauteng Province, Joburg is a melting pot of culture, history, modern urban life, and powerful heritage sites.

As part of your Serorome Valley Ventures journey, guests arrive at OR Tambo International Airport and spend an optional 24-hour stopover in Johannesburg before continuing to Botswana (Francistown). During this stop, guests can refresh at their lodge or hotel, enjoy a meal, and explore iconic locations such as Soweto, Vilakazi Street, and the Hector Pieterson Memorial.

Optional Day 1 Cultural Excursion

Guests are transported from OR Tambo Airport to their accommodation before heading out for a short guided tour. Activities include:

  • Hector Pieterson Memorial (June 16 Soweto Uprising)
  • Vilakazi Street – home of Nelson Mandela & Desmond Tutu
  • Soweto Towers & Bungee Jump viewing
  • Sandton City & Nelson Mandela Square
