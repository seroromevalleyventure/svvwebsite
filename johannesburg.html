Johannesburg 24-Hour Cultural Experience

Johannesburg, proudly known as the City of Gold, is the heartbeat of South Africa and the economic capital of the continent. Located in the vibrant Gauteng Province, Joburg is home to Africa's largest financial district, Sandton, and is a dynamic blend of history, culture, and modern city life.

Guests arrive at OR Tambo International Airport and enjoy a premium 24-hour cultural stopover before heading to Botswana (Francistown). This optional excursion allows travelers to rest, refresh, enjoy a meal, and explore iconic heritage sites at a relaxed pace.

Optional Day 1 Cultural Excursion

After settling in at the lodge or hotel, guests may take a short guided city experience featuring:

  • Hector Pieterson Memorial – honoring the June 16 Soweto Uprising
  • Vilakazi Street – the only street where two Nobel Prize Laureates lived (Nelson Mandela & Desmond Tutu)
  • Soweto Towers – iconic bungee jumping landmark
  • Sandton City & Nelson Mandela Square – Africa’s “Wall Street”

US and other North American nations passport holders do not need a visa for tourist or business stays of up to 90 days in South Africa.
