Johannesburg 24-Hour Cultural Experience

Johannesburg—fondly known as the City of Gold—is South Africa’s powerhouse of culture, economics, and heritage. A melting pot of history and modern urban luxury, Joburg welcomes guests with warm hospitality, iconic locations, and unforgettable African vibrance.

Travelers arriving at OR Tambo International Airport may enjoy a special 24-hour cultural stopover before departing to Botswana (Francistown). This experience provides time to refresh, enjoy a meal, and explore some of the most significant heritage sites in South Africa.

Optional Day 1 Cultural Excursion

After check-in, guests may enjoy a guided cultural experience featuring:

  • Hector Pieterson Memorial – powerful tribute to the 1976 Soweto Uprising
  • Vilakazi Street – home of Nelson Mandela & Archbishop Desmond Tutu
  • Soweto Towers – famous bungee jumping site (Leap of Faith!)
  • Sandton City & Mandela Square – luxury shopping & iconic statue

South Africa uses the South African Rand (ZAR). Travelers must use Rands for purchases. Credit/Debit cards (Visa & Mastercard) are accepted at most establishments. Please avoid using ATMs alone—always request escort assistance from your Serorome Valley Ventures guide.
