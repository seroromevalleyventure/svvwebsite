Explore the Mystical Matopos Hills

Discover the mystical landscape of Matopos National Park, famous for its unique granite rock formations and rich cultural heritage. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is home to some of the world's finest rock art and serves as the final resting place of Cecil John Rhodes and the initial grave of King Mzilikazi. Experience close encounters with both black and white rhinos in their natural habitat.
