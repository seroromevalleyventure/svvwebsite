14-Day Southern Africa Safari Itinerary

A day-by-day journey through South Africa, Botswana, Zambia, and Zimbabwe

Arrival Day — Johannesburg, South Africa

Welcome to South Africa

Upon landing at OR Tambo International Airport, guests are welcomed by Serorome Valley Ventures staff and transferred to their Johannesburg lodge.

Included Cultural Experiences (within first 24 hours):

  • • Hector Pieterson Memorial & Museum (Soweto)
  • • Vilakazi Street — Mandela House & Tutu Residence
  • • Sandton Mandela Square — Photo opportunities & leisure time
  • • Lunch at a premium South African restaurant

After your Johannesburg cultural tour, your journey continues by road to Botswana via Gaborone or Francistown, depending on the selected route.

Day 1 — Francistown, Botswana

Arrival & Journey Begin

Guests arriving directly in Botswana begin their tour here. Pick up at Francistown International Airport and travel to Nata for the first night.

Days 2–4 — Chobe National Park, Botswana

Wildlife Safari Experience

Boat cruise along the Chobe River, morning and evening game drives, and sightings of Africa’s largest elephant population.

Days 5–6 — Livingstone, Zambia

Cultural Immersion

Livingstone Museum, Railway Museum visit, Mukuni Village cultural tour, and traditional experiences with the Big Five walk.

Days 7–9 — Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe

Natural Wonder Experience

Victoria Falls guided forest tour, traditional Boma dinner performance, and Moonbow viewing during a full moon.

Days 10–12 — Matopos National Park, Zimbabwe

Historical & Archaeological

Granite hills with 13,000-year-old rock art, the graves of King Mzilikazi and Cecil John Rhodes, and tours of Nswatugi caves.

Day 13 — Khami Ruins, Zimbabwe

UNESCO Cultural Heritage

Explore the ancient capital of the Torwa dynasty with Chinese and European artifacts, intricate stone walls, and deep historical insight.

→ Explore Khami Ruins

Day 14 — Great Zimbabwe & Chapel of St Francis of Assisi

Ancient African Civilizations

Tour the Great Enclosure, Hill Complex, and sacred Zimbabwe Bird. Then visit the serene Chapel of St Francis of Assisi in Masvingo.

→ Discover Great Zimbabwe

Geographical Marvel — The World's Only Quadripoint

Namibia • Botswana • Zambia • Zimbabwe

Stand at the exact location where four countries meet. This once-in-a-lifetime moment is included in your journey.
