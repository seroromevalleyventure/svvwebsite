Detailed Itinerary

Day-by-day breakdown of your Southern African adventure

Day 1 — Francistown, Botswana

Arrival & Journey Begin

Pick up at Francistown International Airport. Travel to Nata for overnight stay (6 hours drive).

Days 2–4 — Chobe National Park, Botswana

Wildlife Safari Experience

3 days of incredible game viewing. Boat cruise along Chobe River, game drives, and encounters with Africa's largest elephant population.

Days 5–6 — Livingstone, Zambia

Cultural Immersion

Visit Livingstone Museum, Mukuni Village cultural tour, Railway Museum, and experience traditional village life with the Big Five walk.

Days 7–9 — Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe

Natural Wonder Experience

Victoria Falls forest tour, traditional Boma dinner with cultural performances, and Moonbow viewing opportunities during full moon.

Days 10–12 — Matopo Hills, Zimbabwe

Historical & Archaeological

Explore granite hills with 13,000-year-old rock art, visit Cecil Rhodes and King Mzilikazi graves, and discover Nswatugi caves.

Geographical Marvel — The World's Only Quadripoint

Namibia • Botswana • Zambia • Zimbabwe

Stand at the extraordinary point where four nations converge — Namibia, Botswana, Zambia, and Zimbabwe. This is the only place on Earth where you can be in four countries simultaneously.

The quadripoint is located at the confluence of the Zambezi and Chobe rivers, where the borders of four sovereign nations meet at a single point. This extraordinary geographical feature is the only one of its kind on Earth.

During your safari journey, you'll have the rare opportunity to stand at this historic convergence point and experience being in four countries at once — a truly unforgettable moment for any traveler.
