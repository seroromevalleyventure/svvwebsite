14-Day Southern Africa Safari Itinerary

A day-by-day journey through Botswana, Zambia, and Zimbabwe

Day 1 — Francistown, Botswana

Arrival & Journey Begin

Pick up at Francistown International Airport. Travel to Nata for overnight stay (6 hours drive).

Days 2–4 — Chobe National Park, Botswana

Wildlife Safari Experience

3 days of incredible game viewing. Boat cruise along Chobe River, game drives, and encounters with Africa's largest elephant population.

Days 5–6 — Livingstone, Zambia

Cultural Immersion

Visit Livingstone Museum, Mukuni Village cultural tour, Railway Museum, and experience traditional village life with the Big Five walk.

Days 7–9 — Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe

Natural Wonder Experience

Victoria Falls forest tour, traditional Boma dinner with cultural performances, and Moonbow viewing opportunities during full moon.

Days 10–12 — Matopos National Park, Zimbabwe

Historical & Archaeological

Explore granite hills with 13,000-year-old rock art, visit Cecil Rhodes and King Mzilikazi graves, and discover Nswatugi caves.

Day 13 — Khami Ruins, Zimbabwe

Archaeological & Cultural Heritage

After Great Zimbabwe was abandoned in the mid-16th century, Khami became the capital of the Torwa dynasty. This UNESCO World Heritage Site showcases intricate dry-stone walls and artifacts from China and Europe, proving it was a key trade center. Discover the second largest stone-built monument in the country and learn about its link to Great Zimbabwe’s legacy.

→ Explore Khami Ruins

Day 14 — Great Zimbabwe Ruins & Chapel of St Francis of Assisi, Zimbabwe

Ancient Kingdoms & Spiritual Heritage

Explore the magnificent ruins of Great Zimbabwe — the largest ancient structure south of the Sahara and once the capital of the Kingdom of Zimbabwe. Visit the Hill Complex, the Great Enclosure, and view the symbolic Zimbabwe Bird. Later, experience the peaceful Chapel of St Francis of Assisi in Masvingo, a colonial-era church that complements your historical exploration.

→ Discover Great Zimbabwe

Geographical Marvel — The World's Only Quadripoint

Namibia • Botswana • Zambia • Zimbabwe

Stand at the extraordinary point where four nations converge — Namibia, Botswana, Zambia, and Zimbabwe. This is the only place on Earth where you can be in four countries simultaneously.

The quadripoint is located at the confluence of the Zambezi and Chobe rivers. During your safari journey, you'll have the rare opportunity to stand at this historic convergence point — a truly unforgettable moment for any traveler.
