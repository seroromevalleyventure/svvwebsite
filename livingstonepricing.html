Livingstone 2026 Pricing & Experiences

Disclaimer: U.S. passport holders and North America passport holders do not need a visa for tourism in Zambia and can stay for up to 90 days without a visa. While the official currency is the Zambian kwacha, US dollars are widely accepted, especially for tourism expenses. Bring small, crisp bills ($1, $5, $10, $20) as change can be difficult for larger denominations.
