Great Zimbabwe Ruins

Zimbabwe | 1 Day | Up to 8 Guests

Explore the magnificent ruins of Great Zimbabwe, the largest ancient structure south of the Sahara Desert. This UNESCO World Heritage Site was once the capital of the Kingdom of Zimbabwe and showcases remarkable stone architecture built without mortar between the 11th and 15th centuries.

Highlights

  • UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Largest ancient structure south of the Sahara
  • Stone architecture from 11th–15th centuries
  • Great Enclosure exploration
  • Hill Complex visit
  • Expert archaeological guides

Chapel of Saint Francis of Assisi

Zimbabwe | Half Day | Up to 8 Guests

The St Francis of Assisi Chapel in Masvingo is a historic colonial-era church located near the Great Zimbabwe ruins. This peaceful chapel represents the region’s religious heritage and offers a tranquil spiritual retreat for travelers exploring Zimbabwe’s ancient treasures.

Highlights

  • Beautiful colonial-era architecture
  • Religious heritage site
  • Peaceful spiritual atmosphere
  • Historical significance to the Masvingo region
  • Perfect complement to Great Zimbabwe visit
  • Traditional Christian worship space
