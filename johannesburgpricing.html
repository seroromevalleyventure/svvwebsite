Johannesburg Stopover Pricing & Travel Advisory

In South Africa, the accepted currency is Rands (ZAR). All monetary transactions should preferably be done in local currency. Credit and debit cards are accepted in some establishments. Travellers should always ask their Serorome Valley Ventures guide to escort them when using ATMs. Costs may vary depending on season, weather, and group size.

Transportation

Van for full-day transport: R4000 / $220 per day

Meals & Refreshments

Meals for a group of 5–7 travelers: R4500 / $250

Destination Fees

Standard sightseeing: $15 per person per destination

  • Hector Pieterson Memorial – 45 minutes
  • Vilakazi Street / Nelson Mandela’s House – 45 minutes
  • Cradle of Humankind – Historical site
  • Sandton & Nelson Mandela Square – 45 minutes

Clients can pick and choose destinations; all travelers in a group must stick together at all times. Some destinations may have separate prices. All travellers should carry local currency, and debit/credit cards (Visa or MasterCard accepted widely).

Optional Refresh & Accommodation

Travellers may choose to refresh and relax. Maximum cost per person: $180. Accommodation must be specified in advance.

How to Use This Section

Select the destinations and services your group wants. Prices are calculated per person for each activity. Your Serorome Valley Ventures guide will assist and ensure your group stays safe and together.
