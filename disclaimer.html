All services are subject to the terms and conditions of the specific third-party provider.

Recommendation: Clients are strongly advised to secure comprehensive travel insurance at the time of booking to cover potential risks, including but not limited to, medical emergencies, cancellations, delays, personal injury, and loss of property.

2. The Role of Your Travel Co-ordinator

Your dedicated Travel Co-ordinator is committed to designing and managing your journey with the highest level of professionalism and care.

Focus on Client Needs: The Co-ordinator works diligently to meet your travel requirements, taking into account reasonableness, client needs, and your pre-agreed budget. Any requests falling outside the confirmed itinerary or scope of service will be handled on a best-effort basis and may incur additional, pre-approved costs.

Limitation of Authority: The Travel Co-ordinator acts on behalf of Serorome Valley Ventures and has defined authority. They are not authorized to make commitments or incur liabilities outside the scope of your confirmed booking agreement.

3. Protection of Personal Information (POPI Act Compliance)

In adherence to the South African Protection of Personal Information Act (POPI Act), Serorome Valley Ventures is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal data.

Bank Details and Financial Information: We treat your sensitive financial information with the utmost security. Serorome Valley Ventures will not request or accept your bank account details (including account number, card numbers, or PINs) via unsecure methods like text message or through initial website forms.

Established Communication Channels: We will only request the necessary financial or banking information required for processing payments and finalizing your booking once a clear client-business interest has been established and confirmed through official email correspondence and/or secure WhatsApp communication from the official numbers listed on our "Contact" page.

Data Usage: Any personal information collected is processed lawfully, only for the purposes necessary to coordinate and execute your premium travel journey (e.g., booking flights, accommodation, and securing necessary permits) and will not be shared with unauthorized third parties.

4. Pricing and Service Tier Notice

To ensure our premium travel services perfectly align with your expectations and budget, we employ a dynamic, client-centric tier system. Please note the following regarding our pricing structure and negotiation process:

Customised Tier System: Serorome Valley Ventures caters to a broad range of traveler needs, from Backpacker requirements to 5-Star Luxury Hotel client expectations. This means every journey is uniquely crafted and priced based on your chosen level of accommodation, security, transport, and inclusions.

No Fixed US Dollar Pricing: Due to the highly customized nature of our services and the variation in costs across different service tiers (e.g., specific lodges, luxury vehicle choices, private security detail levels), we do not publish a fixed price in US Dollars or any other currency.

Negotiation and Budget Alignment: All sales and final pricing are determined through a dedicated consultation and negotiation process. We discuss the cost of your journey within reason and take into account the peak/off-peak travel season for the relevant destinations (Botswana, Zambia, Zimbabwe). This ensures the quoted price accurately reflects the level of service and the current cost of ground services at the time of booking.

Booking Finalisation: A final, agreed-upon price will be confirmed in a detailed quote after the itinerary, service tier, and budget have been mutually discussed and accepted.
