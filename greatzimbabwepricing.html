Masvingo is a small town, and some establishments may not accept credit or debit cards, except for hotels and resorts. Please carry cash at all times in small denominations: $1, $2, $5, $10, $20.

Great Zimbabwe National Monument

  • UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring ancient stone ruins.
  • Divided into Hill Complex, Valley Complex, and Great Enclosure.
  • Museum on-site displays famous Zimbabwe Bird carvings.
  • Guided tours available for an extra US$3–$5.
  • Entry Price: US$15 per adult / US$8 per child.
  • Craft souvenirs: from US$2 up to US$50.
  • Located approximately 30 km southeast of Masvingo town.

Lake Mutirikwi Recreational Park

  • Conservation fees: US$3 per adult for locals, foreigners via ZimParks.
  • Fishing: US$3 per person
  • Walking trails: US$5
  • Game drive: US$15
  • Activities include game drives, guided walks, birdwatching, boat cruises, and picnics.

Other Attractions

  • Shagashe Game Park: 5 km from Masvingo
  • Mucheke Township Market: 2 km outside Masvingo
  • Masvingo Craft Village: handmade crafts & artworks
  • Roselli Gallery: paintings, carvings, handmade fabrics
  • Masvingo Golf Course: 18-hole course

Transport

  • Van rental: US$280 per day
