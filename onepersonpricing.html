14-Day Southern Africa Safari Journey

Arrival — Johannesburg, South Africa

Meet Serorome Valley Ventures staff at OR Tambo International Airport and transfer to your lodge for rest and cultural immersion.

Included within first 24 hours:

  • Hector Pieterson Memorial & Museum (Soweto)
  • Vilakazi Street — Mandela House & Tutu Residence
  • Sandton Mandela Square
  • Lunch at a premium South African restaurant

Day 1 — Francistown, Botswana

Road transfer to Botswana via Gaborone or Francistown. Overnight in Nata.

Days 2–4 — Chobe National Park, Botswana

Boat cruise on the Chobe River, big game viewing, and morning/evening drives.

Days 5–6 — Livingstone, Zambia

Livingstone Museum, Railway Museum, Mukuni Village cultural tour, and Big Five encounter.

Days 7–9 — Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe

Guided rainforest walk, Boma dinner experience, and Moonbow viewing during full moon.

Days 10–12 — Matopos National Park

Rock art sites, heritage tours, and visit to graves of King Mzilikazi and Cecil John Rhodes.

Day 13 — Khami Ruins, Zimbabwe

UNESCO World Heritage Site with stone walls and ancient cultural artifacts.

Day 14 — Great Zimbabwe

Explore the Great Enclosure, Hill Complex, sacred Zimbabwe Bird, and the Chapel of St Francis of Assisi.

Geographical Marvel — The World's Only Quadripoint

Namibia • Botswana • Zambia • Zimbabwe meet at one point — a rare natural formation.
