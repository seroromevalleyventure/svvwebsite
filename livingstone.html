Livingstone & Victoria Falls Experience

Feel the raw energy of "Mosi-oa-Tunya" — The Smoke That Thunders, where the mighty Zambezi plunges into a vast basalt gorge. Watch rainbows shimmer in the mist by day — and witness the rare Moonbow phenomenon at night. Dive into culture with local crafts, the vibrant Mukuni Village, and authentic Boma dining under the stars.

Highlights

  • Panoramic views of Victoria Falls
  • Lunar Rainbow experience (seasonal)
  • Historic Livingstone town and museum tour
  • Visit to Mukuni Village for cultural immersion
  • Traditional Boma dinner & drumming show
  • Adventure options: bungee, rafting, gorge swing
  • Sunset cruise on the Zambezi River
  • Rainforest walk & photography stops
