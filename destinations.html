Pricing & Extras — Key Costs (displayed here)

The site lists key shuttle, meals and excursion charges so visitors can see expected costs. All prices are approximate — contact us for a tailored quote. Note: Dec 1 → Mar 31 is high season and prices will be higher (seasonal surcharge applied).

Johannesburg — OR Tambo Arrival & Day Transfer

Van (per day): R 4,000 (approx. US$ 240) — full-day private van for group transfers and local stops.

Meals (lunch only for group): R 1,800 – R 2,000 total (approx. US$ 50 per person for groups of 5–7).

Group size note: pricing above is typical for groups of 5–7 people. For smaller or larger groups, contact us for a specific quote.

Heritage & Short Site Visits (Soweto)

Hector Pieterson Memorial (approx.): R 1,000 (approx. US$ 55) — typical group charge; alternatively billed at approx. R 900–R 1,100 per group or R 50–R 60 per person depending on guide/entry/crowd control fees and time on site (30 minutes recommended).

Vilakazi Street visit (walking & quick stops): approx. R 900–R 1,100 per group or R 50–R 60 per person for a short 30–45 minute walk and photo stops.

These are approximate site charges and guide/tip costs; exact fees can vary by day and group size.

Travel onward to Botswana

After the Johannesburg day tour, guests are transported to the border/flight to Botswana as requested. Transport charge for cross-border transfer is quoted per trip — contact us for exact routing and quote (vehicle + driver + border formalities).

Seasonal Surcharge (High season)

Dec 1 → Mar 31: high-season increase will apply. Standard example: +25% seasonal surcharge on shuttle and some package rates during these dates. Exact surcharge may vary by service — final price will show at checkout.

Booking & Payments

We will accept online payments. Payments for SVV excursions can be processed via PayPal / bank transfer. Reminder: SOBA Entertainment (South Africa) and Serorome Valley Ventures (Botswana) will have separate business accounts — we can support linking both PayPals later.

We do not publish live checkout on this page — contact us to receive a tailored invoice or book online via the booking page (checkout with PayPal will be added to the site).
