Johannesburg Arrival Experience (Optional)

First stop: Johannesburg OR Tambo Airport. Guests are driven to their hotel or rest site to refresh, enjoy a meal, and explore Johannesburg during their optional 24-hour stopover before travelling to Botswana.

Optional activities include Hector Pieterson Memorial (June 16 Uprising history) and Soweto's famous Vilakazi Street. Approx. 30–45 min per site.

Van (Full Day): R4000 (~$240)

Meals: R1800–R2000 ($50 pp x 5–7 guests)

Site Fees: R1000 ($50–$60 pp)
